Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Media Master ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your information is handled.

2. Information We Collect

Currently, Media Master processes your media files locally on your device. We do not collect, upload, or store your photos, videos, or audio files on our servers for the app's current processing features.

The app may store processed file metadata such as file name, size, processing history and processing date locally on your device.

3. Device Permissions

To provide media processing features, the app may request access to your photos, videos, audio files, camera, microphone and device storage.

These permissions are used only so you can select, process and save your media files.

You may revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings.

4. How We Use Information

Your media files are processed solely to provide features such as:

  • Video Compression
  • Image Compression
  • Video Trimming
  • Audio Extraction
  • Video Speed Adjustment
  • Audio Speed Adjustment

We do not sell or share your personal media files with third parties.

5. Future AI Features

Future versions of Media Master may include optional AI-powered features such as:

  • Voice Enhancement
  • Background Noise Removal
  • Vocal Separation
  • Instrumental Separation

These features may require securely uploading the selected audio file to our servers for processing.

Files will only be uploaded after you choose to use these AI features and only for the purpose of completing the requested processing.

We do not use your media files to train AI models without your explicit consent.

6. Advertising & Analytics

Future versions of the application may display advertisements and use analytics services to improve the app and user experience.

Advertising and analytics providers may collect anonymous information such as:

  • Device Type
  • Advertising ID
  • Operating System
  • App Version
  • Crash Reports
  • Usage Statistics

Your personal media files are never shared with advertisers.

7. Third-Party Services

The app currently uses third-party libraries such as FFmpeg for on-device media processing.

Future versions may integrate cloud AI services, analytics providers or advertising platforms for optional features.

8. Data Retention

Processed files and processing history remain on your device until you delete them.

If future AI features require cloud processing, uploaded files will be stored only for the time required to complete processing and then deleted according to our data retention practices.

9. Children's Privacy

Media Master is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no electronic storage or transmission method is 100% secure.

Please keep backups of important files before processing.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Continued use of the application after updates means you accept the revised policy.
