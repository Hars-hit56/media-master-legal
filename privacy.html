Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Media Master ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your information is handled.

2. Information We Collect

Currently, Media Master processes your media files locally on your device. We do not collect, upload, or store your photos, videos, or audio files on our servers for the app's current processing features. The app may store processed file metadata (such as file name, size, processing history, and date) locally on your device.

3. Device Permissions

To provide media processing features, the app may request access to your photos, videos, audio files, camera, microphone, and device storage. These permissions are used only to allow you to select, process, and save your media files. You can revoke permissions at any time from your device settings.

4. How We Use Information

Your media files are processed solely to provide features such as video compression, image compression, video trimming, audio extraction, speed adjustment, and other media editing tools. We do not sell or share your personal media files with third parties.

5. Future AI Features

In future versions, Media Master may introduce optional AI-powered features including Voice Enhancement, Background Noise Removal, Vocal Separation, and Instrumental Separation. These features may require securely uploading the selected audio file to our servers for processing. Files will only be uploaded when you choose to use these AI features and only for the purpose of completing the requested processing. We do not use your media files to train AI models without your explicit consent.

6. Advertising & Analytics

Future versions of the app may display advertisements and use analytics services to improve app performance and user experience. Advertising and analytics providers may collect anonymous information such as device type, advertising ID, app version, crash reports, and usage statistics. Your personal media files are never shared with advertisers.

7. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party libraries such as FFmpeg for on-device media processing. Future versions may also integrate cloud AI services, advertising platforms, or analytics providers to support optional AI features and improve the application.

8. Data Retention

Processed files and processing history remain stored on your device until you delete them. If future AI features require cloud processing, uploaded files will be retained only for the time necessary to complete processing and will then be deleted according to our data retention practices.

9. Children's Privacy

Media Master is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your information. While we strive to keep your data secure, no method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure. We recommend keeping backups of important files before processing.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the policy within the app and, where applicable, on our website. Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the support information provided in the app or on our Google Play Store listing.
