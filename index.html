About Media Master

Media Master is a lightweight mobile application designed to help users compress, edit and manage videos, audio and images directly on their devices.

Your privacy matters. Current media processing features operate locally on your device. Future AI-powered features may securely process selected files on our servers only after you choose to use those features.
